Who we are?

We provide full range of Premium Industrial
Services for your business

Established in 1987, Metaweld Sdn Bhd has a 35 year history of business in the Oil & Gas Industry, and a trusted and reputable partner of many Bruneian companies. As a proven and reliable service provider, our clients take pride and the confidence that we can execute and deliver the works to their requirements as entrusted to us.

The expertise and competence in our work come from the years of experience and the extensive knowledge we have of the local markets. With strong continuing support from our OEM Partners, we are well-equipped with the capabilities and access to an extensive range of products, ensuring that our clients have the solutions that best fit their requirements.
